Peksiw Paniki

Asim Kilig Paniki

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces fish
  • 1 knob ginger
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 small bitter gourd
  • 3 pieces finger chili
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

Steps

  • Step 1

    Heat a pan and cooking pot then pour-in vinegar and water.

  • Step 2

    Add salt and whole peppercorn then stir. Bring to a boil.

  • Step 3

    Arrange the fish in the pan along with the ginger, garlic, onion,
    bitter gourd, and finger chili. Cover and simmer in low to medium
    heat for 12 to 15 minutes.

  • Step 4

    Turn off the heat and transfer to a serving plate.

  • Step 5

    Serve hot with steamed rice. Share and enjoy!

  • Step 6

    Note: You may substitute salt with 3/4 to 1 tablespoon fish sauce.
