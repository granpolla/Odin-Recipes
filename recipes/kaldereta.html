Kalderetang Aso

Yummy Kalderetang Doggy

Ingredients

  • 1 kg. beef brisket
  • ¼ pc. onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 Tbsp. liver spread
  • 1 (45g) pack Knorr Complete Recipe
    Mix Kaldereta dissolved in 2 1/2cups water
  • 1 pc. red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper diced
  • 1 piece potato diced
  • 1 pc carrot quartered
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 piece siling labuyo

Steps

  • Step 1

    Let's begin by frying the beef in a hot pot with a little bit of oil.
    Set this aside then throw in the garlic and onions. Return the fried
    meat back to the pot and add in the water. Bring to a boil, then simmer
    until meat becomes tender.

  • Step 2

    Mix in liver spread and Knorr Complete Recipe Matrix Kaldereta
    (mixed in water). Boil for 2 minutes.

  • Step 3

    Add in vegetables and simmer until sauce thickens.
    You can now enjoy Kaldereta any time you please.
    With Knorr Kaldereta Complete Recipe Mix, cooking
    becomes more easy.
